Why we exist

SilimaSoftware was established in 2021 to produce software products and offer development services to startups and small to medium businesses. Our mission is to provide inclusivity to those that otherwise would not be able to afford the exorbitant expenses that can incur on your business when commissioning custom development.

What we do

We are a small team of dedicated, passionate, hard-working individuals who love what we do. We go beyond designing and building software. Our priorities are connection, comprehension and transparency. Ensuring we understand you, your industry and your requirements and keeping you in the loop at every phase of the development cycle.
